How It Works

FOUR STEPS TO THE REAL NUMBER

01

Ingest

We pull published ratings, review text, timestamps, reviewer histories, and posted wait times across Google, Yelp, TripAdvisor, and theme park apps simultaneously.

02

Detect

37+ AI-powered detection signals analyze velocity patterns, linguistic fingerprints, reviewer network clustering, geographic anomalies, and AI-generated text markers.

03

Normalize

Suspicious reviews are removed or down-weighted. Wait time inflation factors are calculated against crowdsourced observations and throughput models.

04

Output

A Value Rating™, Credibility Score, Confidence Band, and Distortion Type tag — the score that should have been there all along.
